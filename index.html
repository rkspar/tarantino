About GC Auto

GC Automotive & Performance is a full-service preventive maintenance and automotive repair center that has been performing high quality, guaranteed automotive repairs and performance upgrades in the Jamesburg, NJ area since 2001.

We service and repair Engine all makes and models of domestic and import vehicles. We are your logical alternative to the dealership for all scheduled maintenance of your personal vehicle, or fleet car or truck. Bring in your foreign and domestic auto, SUV, or pickup today with complete confidence that your vehicle will be serviced correctly while maintaining your manufacturer's warranty.

GC Automotive & Performance uses the latest diagnostic equipment to guarantee your vehicle is repaired or serviced properly and in a timely fashion. We only use quality replacement parts, and our technicians are ASE-certified. Stop in today!

We do all automotive repairs, all makes and models foreign & domestic. Custom installs, and performance install repairs. We will install customer supplied parts or we can order you the parts that you would need.
