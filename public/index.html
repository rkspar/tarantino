About Maxi's

Honest | Professional | Afforadable

Maxi's Auto Shop is a trusted auto repair shop that offers prompt service and honest vehicle assessments. Our locally owned and operated business has experience working on all types of vehicles in Coral Gables. We'll keep your car or truck safe and comfortable year after year. We're here to serve our customers Monday through Friday, 8:00am - 5:00pm, and Saturday, 8:00am - 1:00pm.

Our extensive list of services includes: Oil changes, Auto battery replacements, Auto AC and heat repairs

We'll give you exceptional service whether you need a brake fluid change, engine replacement, or a wheel alignment. No matter how badly your vehicle seems to be driving, it'll be fixed quickly and reliably when you bring it to us.
